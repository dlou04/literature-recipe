Cucumber Sandwiches

Here are the Listed Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Cucumbers
  • Cream Cheese
  • Mayonnaise
  • Lemon juice
  • Seasonings such as Garlic powder, Kosher salt, and black pepper

Here are the listed steps

  1. Taking the bread to your liking, remove the crusts of the bread
  2. With the amount of cucumber that caters to your liking, slice them into fitting amounts
  3. Utilizing the Cream Cheese, Mayonnaise, Lemon Juice, and Seasonings, make your spread
  4. After setting aside the spread, grab a butter knife
  5. Using the butter knife, spread the Spread made onto the insides of the uncrusted bread slices
  6. Place the cucumber slices within the bread slices
  7. Using a more sharp but carefully examined knife, make cuts into the two bread slices that resemble a cube
  8. And TADA you now have cucumber sandwiches to enjoy
